Working Process

Our Process: From Concept to Concrete

Step 01

Project Initiation

We receive the inquiry and our team of engineers studies the requirements. Based on factors like product type (e.g., FT flume drain, U-shape drain, kerb stones, paver blocks, or box culverts), we suggest optimal solutions to meet the client's needs.

Step 02

Design and Planning

Once the client confirms, we begin the mould design and fabrication process. Simultaneously, our factory team prepares and plans for production, considering the specific precast concrete product requirements and the client's timeline.

Step 03

Manufacturing

Full-scale production begins, including casting the concrete products. After casting, the products are moved to a curing area where they undergo a 2-3 week curing process using sprinkler systems and gunny bags as necessary.

Step 04

Quality Control and Delivery

We conduct thorough inspections to ensure product quality. After client approval, we load the precast concrete products onto trucks for delivery. For larger projects, we can begin installation after the first few deliveries while continuing to manufacture and deliver the remaining products.
